2. Standard License

The Standard License is designed for commercial use on a single end product. This license is ideal for freelancers, small businesses, and developers creating a specific application or website.

What you can do:

  • Use the template in a single end product (website, application, etc.)
  • Modify the template to suit your needs

  • Receive lifetime updates for the template

What you cannot do:

  • Use the template in multiple end products without purchasing additional licenses
  • Redistribute the template as-is or with modifications
  • Include the template in a product that allows users to build their own applications
  • Use the template in a SaaS (Software as a Service) application where the template is the primary value

The Extended License provides the most flexibility for commercial use. This license is perfect for agencies, enterprises, and developers creating multiple products or SaaS applications.

What you can do:

License FAQ

Can I use a free template for a client project?

No, free templates are for personal and educational use only. For client projects, you need to purchase at least a Standard License.

Do I need to purchase a license for each client project?

With a Standard License, you need to purchase a separate license for each end product. With an Extended License, you can use the template in multiple end products.

Can I include the template in a product that I sell?

With a Standard License, you can include the template in a single end product that you sell to a single client. With an Extended License, you can include the template in products that you sell to multiple customers.

Do I need to disclose the use of your templates to my clients?

No, you are not required to disclose the use of our templates to your clients. However, you must not claim ownership or authorship of the original template.

What happens if I violate the license terms?

Violation of license terms may result in termination of your license, legal action, and/or additional fees. We encourage you to contact us if you have any questions about license compliance.
